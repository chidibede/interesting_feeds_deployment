Grading Method

The grading Method was based on the number of interactions a post has. The interactions includes : Comments, Likes, Loves and Replies. Comments were ranked highest because it indicates high interest in a post. Loves were ranked next followed by Likes and then Replies. In the lucid blog, some of the most interacted posts were test posts by the developers of Lucid blog. A filteration method was done to the posts returned to account for test posts. Posts with short lengths were also removed leaving behind top posts with longer length.
